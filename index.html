My Unofficial Local Guide to Brooklyn

Brooklyn Paramount

One of the Best new venues in Brooklyn

This is a classic concert venue in downtown brooklyn,
It hosts many different types of artists of all popularities, and has a old theater look to it.



Any Deli That's not Gentrified

These delis usually feature a cat, very delicous food and a vast menu.
A lot of delis now are skyrocketing prices while cheaping out on food costs,
These delis stay true and will always get you the meal you need, for a very reasonable price.



Prospect Park

This is the second largest park in brooklyn (Behind marine park),
There are parts of this park where you are surrounded in 360 by straight woods,
making this park feel almost like your away from a whole city.

The secluded woods areas alongside the beautiful bodies of water land this park a spot on the list.
